About Us

We at zero hunger are aimed at eliminating hunger in our communities, one day at a time!

We do this this with the Aim of reducing hunger induced crimes and killings as well as prevent malnourishment of indigent kids in the society due to lack of good food and proper nutrition

Can’t Donate Food?
Don’t Worry, You can Still Be of Help.

We absolutely understand if you aren’t one for stocking up on foodstuff regularly, there is another way you can bless someone, through your money! Our organisation also makes provision for cash donations. This way, we make sure no one gets left out.

Eliminate hunger, one donation at a
time!

Due to the ever rising inflation in the nigerian economy, a high percentage of the population find it increasingly difficult to feed as well as they should. We at the zero hunger initiative have found that sharing from what we have, no matter how little, helps to make our world a better place.
