zero hunger initiative

We at Zero Hunger Initiative aim at reducing hunger in ourcommunities a day at a time.

We do this with the aim of reducing hunger induced crimes and killings as well as prevent malnourishment of indigent kids in the society due to lack of food and proper nutrition.

Zero Hunger hpes that in 10 years, with the help of you, our wonderful donor, we will be able to completely eradicate hunger in our communities.

our mission

Our aim is to reduce hunger and death in Africa.

Our mission can also be sub divided into two:

  • To do our bit in the sustainment development goals of our nation, Nigeria.
  • Have drop off points in all local government closer to the people who are in dire need of food.

Our vision

We strive to eliminate hunger all over africa and ensure that no one goes to bed hungry.
